About me

ALL ABOUT ME IN 5 THINGS

  1. Feel

    After over 25 years of overthinking about almost everything in my life, I got tired and decided to change the way my instinct works from thinking to feeling. From those moments, feelings and emotions have become key components of all my activities. My life is much easier even though those feelings and emotions can go wrong sometimes and affect my stuff a bit, but thanks to it, I have had a chance to learn to control my emotions better.

  2. Complexity is made up of simple things. So, make it simple. - Van Phung

    To me, life is very simple but day by day, it seems that people are making it more complicated with their thoughts and actions. And with the advancement of the Internet, it is getting worse and worse because many people are looking at the complicated actions of others and getting carried away to not notice the simpler things.
